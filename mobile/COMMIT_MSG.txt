Mobile v1.29: Discover — already-downloaded ticks, Follow-artist + new-upload shelves, search filters + playlists

C) Already-downloaded indicator: DownloadedIndex (a live, normalized index of
   library filenames). Every Discover/search card now shows a GREEN CHECK when the
   item is already in your library, vs the download arrow otherwise. Reactive — it's
   refreshed on Discover open + app resume and the moment a download finishes
   (Downloads -> DownloadedIndex.add); delete the file and the tick reverts.

A) Follow Artist + new uploads: Follows store (follows.json) + a ⭐ on every channel
   result. Followed artists get a "🆕 New from <artist>" shelf at the top of Discover,
   built from their uploads playlist (cheap — 2 quota units, cached 6h). (Background
   push notifications come with the Phase E foreground/background service.)

B) Search power-ups:
   - Playlists now appear in search results → tap to open in the Channel tab and
     bulk-download the whole playlist (yt-dlp, no quota).
   - Sort: Relevance / Newest / Most viewed (order=relevance|date|viewCount).
   - "Official only" filter (heuristic: Official titles + VEVO / "- Topic" channels).
