Mobile v1.25: fix MP4 video stutter (force H.264 1080p30 + decoder fallback)

The video kept stuttering ("trying to catch up" while audio stayed smooth) — the
classic symptom of the hardware decoder dropping frames on a too-demanding stream.
Since PlaybackService runs in-process, it was never a routing/IPC problem; it was
the stream itself (high-bitrate 1080p60, or VP9/AV1).

- Download: force avc1 (H.264) video + AAC audio, capped at 1080p and 30fps
  (720p/480p likewise). These decode in hardware smoothly and stay A/V-synced.
- Player: ExoPlayer now uses DefaultRenderersFactory with decoder fallback, so a
  struggling primary decoder falls back instead of stuttering.

Note: applies to NEW downloads. Videos grabbed before this need re-downloading to
pick up the H.264/30fps format.
